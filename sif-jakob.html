Sif Jakob

Sif Jakobs is the woman behind the jewelry and the company Sif Jakobs Jewellery.

She was born and raised in Iceland, trained as a goldsmith in Sweden and traveled widely throughout the world. Copenhagen has been her base since 2000 and Italy has for many years been her second home. The love of Italian elegance is a constant source of inspiration and the reason for her collections all carry Italian city names.

Sif Jakobs has always been artistic landscaped and has all his life burned to create. Her lifestyle, effervescent personality and instinctive sense of quality and design contribute to her glamorous design that provides everyday life a touch of luxury.

"Travel is my inspiration - I love to see what is happening around the world and be inspired by fashion right now. I want to create jewelry that gives a sense of luxury. A simple and beautiful style - every single day."
