Services

Jewellery repairs

Here at G.W.Pack we can do simple repairs to jewellery in our small work shop and offer repairs by our goldsmith within a week. Along with estimates we can offer the following –

  • Ring resizing claw replacement and retipping
  • stone replacement and stone resetting
  • general jewellery repairs
  • Pearl restringing
  • hand and seal engraving
  • valuations for insurance & probate.

Watch repairs

Our trained competent staff can fit all types of watch battery's while you wait.

In some cases we use a local watchmaker. If you require a service or parts to your watch we can accommodate this for you also with estimates.

Engraving

We can supply trophies to order, catalogues are available. We also offer an engraving service usually within a week and have been using the same engraving machine for 60 years as it's part of our family business.
