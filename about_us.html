About Us

G. W. Pack are a family run jewellers founded in 1875 by Jacob Pack. The current owners are sisters Rachel Gibson and Lucy Batten who are the fourth generation of the family to run the business.

Geoff Pack and his wife Jenny inherited the business from Geoffâ€™s parents in 1968. They expanded the business purchasing a second shop in Gold Street and ran both shops for many years.

In 1993 a "Great Fire" starting in the adjoining premises destroyed the Bridge Street shop. The building was rebuilt, and reopened in November 1995. The Gold Street premises were subsequently closed and the business was then run successfully from Bridge Street with Geoff retiring in 1997.

In 2008 Rachel and Lucy took the bold decision to move to a prime location within the town centre despite the general economic downturn and the business has seen good growth ever since.

The company has now been trading 140 years and are proud to be one of the oldest businesses in Tiverton possibly the South West!

G.W.Pack is proud of its knowledgeable, dedicated staff whose customer service is second to none. Fran is the longest serving having completed 39 years and all 6 staff have 121 years of experience between them.

Rachel, Lucy and their staff would like to welcome you to their newly refurbished shop where their branded jewellery has now been expanded giving customers a greater choice while still maintaining the company's core values.

G.W. Pack always strive to give exceptional service to every customer who pays them a visit.
